    UB Science High Online Application Form

    Greetings from the University of Baguio Science High School, where great minds meet!

    As one of the pioneers of the Science Education Curriculum in the country, the University of Baguio Science High School serves as a second home to scholars who excel in different fields such as Science, Math, Research, Sports, and Performing Arts. Every year, hundreds of aspiring students take the Science High Qualifying Test to see if they are qualified to be a part of our institution.

    Because of the current Enhanced Community Quarantine due to the COVID-19 pandemic, the qualifying assessment of incoming Grade 7, Grade 8, and Grade 11 students for SY 2021-2022 are made online. This application form, together with all the requirements, the essay, the recommendation form, and the Interview will determine an applicant's eligibility for admission in UB Science High School for SY 2021-2022. Kindly read carefully all the information asked in this form and respond accordingly. Invalid responses and documents submitted may affect your application to UB Science High School.

    Please take note that the personal information asked in this form is necessary for the application process and will be handled with the utmost care and confidentiality, based on the Data Privacy Policies of the University of Baguio. However, please be reminded that there are certain limitations in terms of keeping the data private because of the use of technology and the internet.

    Thank you for considering UB Science High School.
